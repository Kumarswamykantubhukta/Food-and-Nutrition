Brief Understanding about Nutrition

Maintaining good nutrition is essential for overall health and well-being. By understanding the roles of different nutrients, adhering to dietary guidelines, and making informed food choices, individuals can enhance their quality of life and reduce the risk of numerous health issues.

let food be the medicine not medicine be the food

    Carbohydrates

  • Function: Primary source of energy.
  • Types: Simple (sugars) and complex (starches and fibers).
  • Sources: Fruits, vegetables, grains, legumes.
    Proteins

  • Function: Building and repairing tissues, making enzymes and hormones.
  • Sources: Meat, fish, dairy, beans, nuts, seeds.
    Fats

  • Function: Energy storage, protecting organs, supporting cell growth.
  • Types: Saturated, unsaturated (monounsaturated and polyunsaturated), and trans fats.
  • Sources: Oils, butter, avocados, nuts, seeds, fatty fish.
